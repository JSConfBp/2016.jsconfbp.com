I love music since I can remember. I love to play the drums. I love to attend music festivals. I love to listen music on SoundCloud. But what I love the most is the Web, because it offers everything we need to be creative with music:

* Compose and produce music in the browser * Use external MIDI hardware like a Drumpad, mini Piano or DJ-Controller * Create real-time music visualizations * Perform a live show and interact with the audience"
